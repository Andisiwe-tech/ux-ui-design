Homemade Burger

Burgeris a dellicious dish consisting of fillings-usually a patty of ground meat,typically beef- placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll. the patties often served with cheesse, lettuce,tomato, onions, bacon with condiments such as mastard, mayonnaise and others.

Burger is super special to me as a dish, because of the softiness in it and that juicy spark from its condiments, and with the patties which the burger consists makes it super tastey and enjoyable to me.

Version of the meal have been served forover a century, but its origins are still unclear. The 1758 edition of the book The Art of Cookery Made in Plain and Easy by Hnnah Glasses included a recipe called "Hamburgh sausages", suggesting that it should be served 'roasted with toasted bread under it'.
